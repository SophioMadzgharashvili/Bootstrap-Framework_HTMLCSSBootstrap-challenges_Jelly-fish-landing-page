Jelly Fish

jellyfish aren't actually fish they're invertebrates, or animals
with no backbones. Jellyfish have tiny stinging cells in their
tentacles to stun or paralyze their prey before they eat them.
Inside their bell-shaped body is an opening that is its mouth.
